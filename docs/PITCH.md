# ProofPulse pitch

## 10-second pitch

ProofPulse is Datadog plus an AI SRE plus on-chain proof for Web3 infrastructure.

## 30-second pitch

Web3 outages are trust events, not just technical events. When an RPC, bridge, validator, or RWA oracle fails, teams need to prove what happened and what evidence supports their response. ProofPulse monitors infrastructure, creates AI-generated incident reports, hashes the evidence, and anchors that proof on-chain so customers and auditors can verify it later.

## 2-minute pitch

Web3 infrastructure increasingly touches real money: payments, stablecoins, tokenized assets, trading, custody, and cross-chain settlement. But the operational stack still depends on dashboards and trust-me status pages.

ProofPulse changes that. It continuously probes critical infrastructure, captures raw observations, uses an AI agent to generate an incident assessment, and creates a canonical evidence bundle. The bundle is hashed and the hash is anchored on-chain. Private logs stay private, but the proof is public and tamper-evident.

This is useful for RPC providers, validators, bridge teams, RWA platforms, and compliance-heavy Web3 startups. It also directly maps to AWS infrastructure: ECS, Lambda, CloudWatch, OpenSearch, S3, KMS, DynamoDB, and Bedrock.

The hackathon prototype already demonstrates the full loop: target registration, synthetic checks, risk analysis, evidence bundle, and prepared attestation.

## What makes it different

- It is not just monitoring: it creates audit artifacts.
- It is not just AI: AI output is tied to evidence hashes.
- It is not just on-chain: only proofs go on-chain, not private logs.
- It is not just Web3-native: it is credible for compliance and enterprise pilots.

## Killer demo sentence

“Here is the incident. Here is the AI explanation. Here is the evidence hash. Here is the on-chain proof that the report existed before we edited the postmortem.”
