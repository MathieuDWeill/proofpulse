# FAQ

## Is this just monitoring?

No. Monitoring tells operators what is happening now. ProofPulse creates a verifiable evidence trail that can be reviewed after the fact by customers, auditors, or counterparties.

## Why put anything on-chain?

To make the existence and integrity of the evidence independently verifiable. The raw evidence remains private; only the hash and metadata pointer are anchored.

## Why AI?

Operators and customers need fast explanations: severity, likely cause, blast radius, recommended actions. AI is useful when it is grounded in collected evidence and produces a reviewable report.

## Why AWS?

This product needs continuous probes, log ingestion, search, encrypted evidence storage, scheduled workflows, and AI inference. AWS is a natural deployment target.

## Who pays?

RPC providers, validator operators, bridge teams, RWA/tokenization platforms, custody providers, DeFi protocols, and Web3 startups preparing for institutional customers.
